Welcome!

This site simulates a balancing change proposed to increase the effectiveness of 0q, 2q, 3q, and 4q lasers while disincentivizing long-range sniping.

The change revolves around making laser beams deal damage in cones instead of in rays, details below.

Left click on the simulation to fire the modified laser, right click to fire the current version of lasers.

Adjust the configuration of the lasers and the armor in the config menu via the gear icon beneath this tab.

Beam Expansion

Laser beams expand to deal damage in a cone, with the tip placed at the end of the optics. The radius of the cone depends on the damage of the shot.

Expansion is measured in damage per meter of expansion per 100m range. If the config value is set to 1000, then a 1000 damage shot will expand to a radius of 1m at 100m, 2m at 200m, etc.
